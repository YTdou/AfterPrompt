# Code of Conduct

AfterPrompt contributors are expected to make participation respectful, constructive, and free from harassment. Be considerate across differences in experience, identity, language, and viewpoint; critique work rather than people; respect privacy; and stop behavior when asked.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, sustained disruption, and publishing another person's private information without permission.

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainers through the repository's private channels. Maintainers may remove content, limit participation, or take other proportionate action. Enforcement decisions should protect reporters' privacy and avoid conflicts of interest.

This policy is informed by the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
